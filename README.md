#HydraX

HydraX is an AI-powered sustainability tool that helps the city of London, UK measure and visualize their rainwater harvesting potential.
By combining open-source environmental data with geospatial analytics, HydraX calculates how much rainwater could be collected from rooftops, visualizing the potential savings in both liters of water and reduced stormwater runoff.
